For Prospective Students

PhD Students

I am always looking for talented students who are excited about machine learning. I recruit from Northeastern CS PhD program. Please read carefully before you email me:

  • Use "NEU prospective PhD student [Your name] - Rose" as your email subject (to help me filter out spams).
  • Read some of my projects and papers, specify how your research interests match mine.
  • I highly value mathematical and programming skills. Please remember to highlight your related experiences (e.g. contests, awards).

Visiting Students

I occasionally host visitors/interns who have strong motivation and interets in machine learning research.

  • Use "NEU visiting student [Your name] - Rose" as your email subject (to help me filter out spams).
  • Specify whether you will need funding/visa support, visit length and highlight your relevant experience.
