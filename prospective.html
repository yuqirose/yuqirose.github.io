For Prospective Students

PhD students

I am always looking for talented students who are excited about machine learning, see opening post. I recruit from Northeastern CS PhD program. Please read carefully before you email me:

  • Email subject: '2017 NEU CS Prospective Student [Your Name]'. List your education backgrounds, GPA, TOFEL/GRE Score and publication records.
  • I highly value mathematical and programming skills. Please remember to highlight your related experiences (e.g. contests, awards).
  • One thing you are super proud of that makes you special :)
  • [Optional] Read some of my research projects and share your research ideas that are related.
