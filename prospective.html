PhD Students

I am always looking for talented PhD students who are excited about machine learning. I recruit from UCSD CSE PhD program. Please read carefully before you email me:

  • Use "UCSD prospective PhD student [Your name] - Rose" as your email subject (to help me filter out spams).
  • Read some of my projects and papers, specify how your research interests match mine.
  • I highly value mathematical and programming skills. Please remember to highlight your related experiences (e.g. contests, awards).

Current UCSD Students

If you are a current UCSD student and want to engage in machine learning research, welcome!

  • Enroll in one of my advanced machine learning courses and do well.
  • Read some of my projects and papers for potential project match.
  • Financial support is available but will be decided based on evidence of commitment and progress.

Visiting Students

I occasionally host visitors/interns who have strong motivation and interests in machine learning research.

  • Use "UCSD visiting student [Your name] - Rose" as your email subject (to help me filter out spams).
  • Specify whether you will need funding/visa support, visit length and highlight your relevant experience.
