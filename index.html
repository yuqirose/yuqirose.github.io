Biography

I am a fifth year Ph.D. candidate of Computer Science in University of Southern California. My advisor is Prof. Yan Liu and Prof. Cyrus Shahabi . Before that, I received the B. Eng degree from Computer Science and Technology Department in Zhejiang University in 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Zhihua Zhang.

I'm generally interested in learning structures from large-scale spatio-temporal data, particularly with tensors. I have expertise in tensor optimization, spatio-temporal analysis, probabilistic graphical models, and anomaly detection. My work has been applied to climate informatics, mobile recommender system and social media anomaly detection. For more information see the links below and my CV.

I'm co-organizing NIPS workshop on Learning with Tensors: Why Now and How? (Tensor-Learn 2016).
